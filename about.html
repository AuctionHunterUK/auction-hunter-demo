About AuctionSavvy

A private demonstration — two tools, one idea

AuctionSavvy is a proof of concept for UK antique dealers and collectors: find what is for sale, see where it is, and decide whether it is worth the trip. The demonstration brings two complementary tools together.

Lots

A regularly updated catalogue of pine furniture and butchers’ blocks appearing in UK auctions, organised into Local, Today and Later. Every item links directly to its original EasyLive Auction listing for full details and bidding.

Map

An interactive map of UK auction houses, colour-coded according to when their next sale takes place. It also includes a route planner for discovering auction houses along a journey.

Planned membership

Subject to an agreed EasyLive partnership and authorised data feed, AuctionSavvy could develop through three planned membership levels.

Planned

Map — Free

Free access to the UK auction-house map, upcoming sale dates and route-planning tools.

Planned

Catalogue — Member

A paid personalised catalogue using each member’s search terms, local auction houses and preferences. Every lot would continue to link directly to EasyLive for details and bidding.

Planned

Wanted — Dealer add-on

An additional sourcing service for dealers handling customer requests. Dealers could type a natural-language request or paste only the relevant product requirements from a customer email. The service would retain the sourcing requirements rather than the original email or unnecessary customer personal details.

An authorised morning process could compare active requests with newly supplied catalogue data and email possible matches containing a photograph, catalogue dimensions, auction house, sale date, reason for the match and a direct EasyLive link.

These are possible matches and dealers must verify the original catalogue information before bidding.

Working responsibly with auction data

All lot and auction information displayed in this demonstration is sourced from EasyLive Auction. Rights in the listings, descriptions and imagery remain with EasyLive, the participating auctioneers and their respective rights holders.

AuctionSavvy is currently a password-protected, invitation-only demonstration created to explore a potential partnership with EasyLive. It is not being offered as a commercial service, and every lot links directly back to EasyLive for viewing and bidding.

We recognise that EasyLive’s published site-usage terms require written permission for automated data collection. Any development beyond this demonstration would therefore be undertaken through an agreed data-access arrangement or partnership. We will promptly remove any information or imagery at the request of EasyLive or the relevant rights holder.

Proposed data-use monitoring

A future partnership could also include an analytics-based monitoring capability designed to help protect catalogue information. It could examine access patterns and flag unusual automated collection, bulk extraction or catalogue reuse for investigation.

This would be a proposed detection and alerting tool—not an automatic accusation or enforcement system—with its rules, thresholds and reporting agreed directly with EasyLive.
