Auction Hunter

A private demonstration — two tools, one idea

Auction Hunter is a proof-of-concept for UK antique dealers and collectors: find what's for sale, see where it is, and decide if it's worth the trip. This demo has two pages, working together.

Lots

A daily feed of pine furniture lots pulled from EasyLive's public listings, organised into Local, Today, and Later. Each lot links straight through to bid.

Map

An interactive map of UK auction houses, colour-coded by when their next sale is, with a route planner for finding houses along a journey.

What's next

The long-term idea is a subscription product: a free map tier, with paid tiers adding personalised search terms, a saved list of local auction houses, and alerts. This demo doesn't build those yet — it's here to show what the product looks like once they exist.

A note for EasyLive

This demo currently pulls publicly listed auction data from EasyLive to show what this kind of product could look like. It isn't a public product — it's invitation-only, not linked from anywhere public, and not intended for anyone outside this conversation.

If this moves beyond a demo, the intention is to work directly with EasyLive on a proper data partnership, rather than continuing to source listings independently.
